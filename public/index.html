Gaps

November 8, 2017 Kevin

  • Twitter

  • Google+

  • Facebook

  • Reddit

  • LinkedIn

  • StumbleUpon

  • Email

I keep coming across the concept of gaps–more specifically the gap between reality and one’s vision for how reality should be. I first came across this concept in the Fifth Discipline by Peter Senge.

Currently I am reading Man’s Search for Meaning by Viktor Frankl.

This concept also came up in this interview of Ray Dalio that I listened to this week.

Frankl argues that mental health requires tension and the essence of being a human being is to close the gap between what is and what should be. More powerfully, Frankl argues that a person needs the struggle for a worthwhile goal to find meaning in life.

I hope my goals and struggles are worthwhile.
