Scheduled Time

October 14, 2017 Kevin

More on the book Deep Work. Cal Newport suggest scheduling every minute of your day. This is challenging–it takes training and practice but it has had profound impact on my productivity and well being. Everyone has a plan ‘til they are punched in the mouth.–Mike Tyson Tasks either take longer or get completed quicker than expected thus there is a lot of adjustment to the schedule but it is well worth the effort.
